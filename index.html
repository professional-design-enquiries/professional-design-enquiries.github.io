  • FAQ

    Your questions made clear

    • Q1. What kind of Apprenticeships do you offer? A1. Business Admin Apprenticeship for 16-18 year olds and 19+, Childcare Apprenticeship for 16-18 year olds and 19+ Customer Service Apprenticeships for 16-18 year olds and 19+, Hairdressing Apprenticeships for 16-18 year olds and 19+ Health and Social Care Apprenticeships for 16-18 year olds and 19+, ICT Apprenticeships for 16-18 year olds and 19+ Teacher Training Apprenticeships for 16-18 year olds and 19+
    • Q2. How long do they last? A2. Between 12-15 months depending on how quick you are to achieve your chosen Diploma and you must make sure to attend Colege once a week.
    • Q3. What are the entry requirements? A3. Employment in your chosen field i.e. if studying Childcare, employed to work within a nursery, your employer must pay a minimum of £95.00 per week. You will be required to attend College once a week to gain knowledge and understanding to carry out assessments, functional skills (English, Maths and ICT) and personal learning and Thinking Skills Level 2 Qualification or GCSE Grades A-D.
